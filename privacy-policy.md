# Privacy Policy — BZ5 Companion

**Effective date: 11 July 2026**

BZ5 Companion ("the app") is an open-source companion app for Toyota/BYD EVs. This policy describes what data the app handles.

## Data the app collects

**Vehicle telemetry.** Battery, charging and trip data read from your vehicle (via the head unit or an OBD2 Bluetooth adapter). Stored **locally on your device** by default.

**Cloud sync (optional, off by default).** If you enable cloud backup, the app sends to our server (carbridge.neardo.work):
- your email address — used only for sign-in via one-time code;
- a device identifier (random UUID and a hardware fingerprint derived from Android's device ID) — used to link backups to your devices;
- your vehicle telemetry and trip history — to back it up and restore it on your devices.

Data in transit is protected with HTTPS.

## What we do NOT do

- No ads, no analytics, no tracking SDKs.
- No location collection.
- No sale or sharing of data with third parties.
- No data leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

## Permissions

- **Bluetooth** — to connect to your OBD2 adapter.
- **Internet / network state** — cloud sync only.
